Hospital infections

Ronald Campbell
CHCF Center for Health Reporting

Beginning in October 2014, the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services began fining hospitals for certain hospital-acquired infections. Hospitals were graded on a scale from 1 (best) to 10 (worst). The worst 25 percent, those with a score of 7 or higher, were docked 1 percent of their Medicare reimbursement for the federal 2014-2015 fiscal year.
